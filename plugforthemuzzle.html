Plug for the Muzzle of a Gun

today is a cue to leap up & prove
my dedication to the basics:
hygiene, dryness,
unfettered movement

to forget the thump
of satisfaction, of rhythm
making itself public, to feel
no relief at silent zygote

instead slim pink athletic
go & do unstoppable body
subordinate to fabric, as if
protecting a swatch from stain

could keep a crease on thigh
clean, so I might forgo
the opportunity to touch
my own blood without feeling

with an index finger I flatten
the clot into the sheet
convinced for a moment not
to move and not to be guided
