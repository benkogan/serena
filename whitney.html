Whitney


When did this expression eat each of your other expressions
such that eyes wide with care set broad & wilder

Before you I assumed intricacy & style but I was mistaken
you know by heart schematics I expected to push you over

like froth on stone Whitney the use of first name
mining an interlude face mightn't reflect

I was glad to have heard you can outmaneuver fear
with a workbook of scenes ranked hierarchically by stress

Safety is not confidentiality or grace or a question
for a woman I won't speak to again Whitney

I begged you to say this speculum this gristle
& pinching bordered with heat might like lead

be recalled beneath the paper this body might be
outside the myth your body tells you Whitney

that as a woman you might understand women
when they speak or that each little incapacity

might be one and the same Whitney my spectrum
of exposure my reminder of sanctuary eating lunch

at your desk Whitney the myth I know by heart what hurts
is not the opening but the gaze Whitney you do good

& amusing work your face splayed open so proudly
it could camouflage the disarray of power in your hands

With thanks to Quarto.
