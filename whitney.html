Whitney

Your first name forces an interlude
well-trained face won't reflect
eyes midday over lid horizon

This expression eats each
of your other expressions until
eyes wide with care turn glassy

Between the reprimand of ye-s
and a cautious breeze that's me
I register we might be close in age

The myth about one woman
understanding another patterns
your calendar checker print

You claim you can oust fear
with a workbook of scenes
ranked hierarchically by stress

Is sanctuary only patience
Are all incapacities one
and the same Whitney

Our bodies a spectrum
of exposure under paper
heat pinching gristle

After work you eat a salad
unbothered by the disarray
of power in your hands

With thanks to Quarto.
